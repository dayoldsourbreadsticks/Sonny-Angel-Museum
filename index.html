Sonny Angel PVC | Designed by Dreams Inc. Sonny Angel is a small, cherubic figurine known for its distinctive wide-eyed expression and whimsical design. First introduced by the Japanese company Dreams Inc., these collectible figures are often adorned with themed headdresses, ranging from animals and fruits to seasonal motifs. Sold in blind box packaging, each Sonny Angel is a surprise, adding an element of discovery to the collecting experience. Over time, these figures have gained international popularity, captivating collectors with their nostalgic charm and playful aesthetic.

Sonny Angel: Lop Ear Rabbit
Animal Series | Designed by Dreams Inc.
The Animal Series is one of the most beloved Sonny Angel collections, featuring charming creatures that evoke a sense of nostalgia and delight. This particular figure, the Lop Ear Rabbit, captures the gentle and endearing nature of its real-life counterpart, making it a cherished addition for collectors. With its signature wide-eyed expression and playful stance, the Lop Ear Rabbit embodies the whimsical essence of Sonny Angel figurines, bringing a touch of warmth and joy to any display.


Sonny Angel Hippers are a fun and creative twist on the classic Sonny Angel figurines, bringing a new way to display these adorable collectibles. Unlike traditional Sonny Angels, which stand upright on flat surfaces, Hippers are designed to stick onto a variety of places, adding a playful and whimsical touch to your surroundings. Each Hipper comes with an adhesive pad on its lower back, allowing it to securely "hip" onto surfaces like the edge of a desk, a computer monitor, a bookshelf, or even the cover of a book. This unique design makes it look as though the little figurine is peeking out or playfully hanging on, adding a bit of charm and surprise wherever you place it. Whether you're a longtime Sonny Angel collector or new to the world of these cute figures, Hippers offer a fresh and interactive way to enjoy them. Plus, their compact size and secure grip make them easy to place and reposition, letting you get creative with where you display your favorite characters.
